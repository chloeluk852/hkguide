Attractions in Hong Kong

Dragons Back Hiking Trail

Description: Dragon's Back is a stunning hiking trail in Hong Kong that offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape. This relatively easy hike takes you along a ridge that resembles the back of a dragon, providing panoramic vistas of the South China Sea, lush greenery, and the iconic Hong Kong skyline in the distance.

Trail Details: As you walk the trail, you’ll encounter a mix of shaded paths and open areas, making it a pleasant journey for both novice and experienced hikers. The trail is well-marked, and you can complete it in about 1.5 to 2 hours, depending on your pace.

Best Time to Visit: To make the most of your visit, consider going early in the morning or late in the afternoon to avoid the heat and enjoy the sunrise or sunset views. Don’t forget to bring plenty of water and wear comfortable shoes.

Location: Latitude: 22.236927175029958, Longitude: 114.24322572941038
