Attractions in Hong Kong

Tian Tan Buddha (Big Buddha)

Description: The Tian Tan Buddha, or Big Buddha, is a striking 26-meter bronze statue located on Lantau Island in Hong Kong. Symbolizing harmony between man and nature, it is a major pilgrimage site for Buddhists. Visitors can climb 268 steps to reach the statue, enjoying stunning views along the way. At the base, there's a small temple and exhibitions that offer insights into Buddhist teachings.

Tips: To fully appreciate your visit, arrive early to avoid crowds and explore the nearby Po Lin Monastery. The Tian Tan Buddha is not only a breathtaking sight but also a meaningful place for reflection and cultural appreciation.
