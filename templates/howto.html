How to Use HKGUIDE

1. Exploring the Map

Navigate through Hong Kong using our interactive map. You can:

  • Zoom in/out using mouse wheel or pinch gestures
  • Click and drag to move around the map
  • Click the location icon to center on your current position

2. Finding Locations

Look for markers on the map indicating points of interest. Each marker represents a unique location worth visiting in Hong Kong.

Tip: Click on any marker to view detailed information about that location.

3. User Account

Sign in with your Google account to:

  • Save your favorite locations
  • Get personalized recommendations
  • Access additional features

4. Location Details

When you click a location, you'll find:

  • Description of the location
  • Tips for visiting
  • Photos and additional information
  • Directions and accessibility details
Pro Tip: Use the current location button in the bottom right corner to quickly find attractions near you!
