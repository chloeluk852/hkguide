Attractions in Hong Kong

Tai O Fishing Village

Description: Tai O Fishing Village is a charming and picturesque destination located on Lantau Island, offering a glimpse into Hong Kong's traditional fishing culture. Known for its stilt houses and vibrant local life, Tai O is a unique contrast to the bustling city atmosphere. As you wander through the narrow streets, you'll encounter fishermen selling fresh seafood, local markets, and quaint shops offering handmade souvenirs. One of the highlights of Tai O is its stunning natural surroundings, including beautiful beaches and scenic hiking trails. You can also take a boat ride to explore the nearby waterways, where you might spot the rare pink dolphins that inhabit the waters.

Tips: To fully enjoy your visit, consider going early in the day to avoid crowds and soak in the peaceful ambiance. Sampling local delicacies, such as shrimp paste and egg waffles, is a must. Tai O offers a delightful opportunity to experience Hong Kong’s heritage and enjoy the tranquil beauty of its coastal landscapes, making it a perfect day trip for both locals and visitors alike!

How to get there: Directions from MTR Shau Kei Wan Station to Dragon’s Back:
1. Exit MTR Shau Kei Wan Station via Exit A2.
2. Walk to the Shau Kei Wan Bus Terminus.
3. Board bus number 9 at the terminus.
4. Alight at the To Tei Wan stop on Shek O Road.
